About Me

My name is Juliette Gale and I am a recent graduate from Clark University with a B.A. in both Geography and Global Environmental Studies. I am currently completing my Masters in Geographic Information Science at through Clark University's Accelerated Degree Program. I am also working as a remote sensing research assistant at Clark Labs, and as an undergraduate GIS tutor through the Clark University Geography Department.

Apart from working on GIS projects, I am a member of the Clark University women's rowing team, and spend my additional free time hiking, gardening, woodworking.

I am extremely passionate about the endless ways GIS and mapping can be used for scientific communication, and will be updating my contributions on my online portfolio.
